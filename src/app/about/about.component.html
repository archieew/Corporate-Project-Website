About

We believe in transparacy cooperation globally, and the free exchange of talents and ideas. A forum for the sharing for these technical and humanitarian discussions is given for meetings, conferences and other events. We are also committed to creating a secure, constructive and supportive atmosphere for all participants in conferences, activities and meetings who participate in these discussions with us.

Conferences are a part of the legacy of this organization and continue today to be a catalyst for creativity. We are comitted to developing strategies that support and expand the conference activities throughout the world.
